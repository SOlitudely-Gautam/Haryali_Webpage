Scientific Name

Piper longum.

Useful Parts

fruits (spikes) and roots

Uses

digestive and respiratory support, and as a potential anti-aging agent.

Location

commonly found in the tropical rainforests of the country, particularly in the Khasi hills, lower hills of West Bengal, and the evergreen forests of the Western Ghats.

Taste & Potency

Taste:more like a mix of spices than a single plant
Potency:potent herb in Ayurvedic medicine,
Metabolic Property:anticancer properties due to antioxidant and its inhibitory action on inflammatory mediators like cytokines and TNF-α
